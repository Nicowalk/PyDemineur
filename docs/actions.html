Reveal the case that is selected.

Parameters:

  • row_selection (int) – Selected row by the user.

  • column_selection (int) – Selected column by the user.

  • number_of_rows (int) – Total number of rows in the board.

  • number_of_columns (int) – Total number of columns in the board.

  • board (list of list of int) – 2D list containing integers with the number of mines around each case.

  • list_of_mines (list of tuple of int) – List of positions (row, col) of the mines.

  • cases_revealed (list of tuple of int) – List of the positions (row, col) of revealed cases.
